Idli

This light fluffy pancake made of rice and urad dal is the perfect way to start your day.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups basmati rice
  • 1.5 tablespoons of fenugreek seeds
  • 5 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 1.5 cups of urad dal
  • Salt as required
  • Water as required

Instructions

  1. Soak the rice & dal and grind into a paste & combine
  2. Let the batter ferment, then add salt
  3. Transfer the idli batter in the idli stand
  4. Wait for the utensil to cool a bit before scooping the idlis out
